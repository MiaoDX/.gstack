## Vision

### 10x Check
The real opportunity is not “a nicer proof-pack page.” It is a reusable
phase-review contract that lets RoboWBC explain staged policy behavior in a way
humans and downstream tools can both trust. The current velocity showcase is
the first instance, not the product boundary.

### Platonic Ideal
Any policy run can publish a versioned phase-review artifact that says what the
robot was supposed to do, what phase it was in, what evidence was captured, and
how to inspect lag or anomalies without reverse-engineering the run from raw
frames.
